PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

The Mega Drive also known as Sega Genesis was Sega's most successful console, which according to the same, had around 40 million units sold worldwide and had Nintendo's SNES as its biggest competitor. Among the most successful games on the Sega console was Sonic Hedgehog, the fast hedgehog adventurer well known in the gaming world. The control of the Mega Drive has 6 buttons on the right side, they are at the top (X, Y and Z), at the bottom (A, B and C), in the center is located the Start button and on the left side the control has a directional cross. Bring nostalgia into your home by reliving or playing the great hits that marked the era for the first time.
