PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

In 1994, the console known as Neo Geo CD came to the world. The console created by SNK has a CD system that replaces cartridges, which guarantees cheaper games and a better sound system than the other 4th generation consoles. Thanks to its sophisticated CD player for the time, the console was exciting to read several music CDs. The Neo Geo CD has an enviable library of games, due to its almost perfect uncertainties, owing only in terms of loading. In addition, the fact that the Neo Geo had the largest RAM memory of the other consoles at the time helped it to continue selling games for a long time.
